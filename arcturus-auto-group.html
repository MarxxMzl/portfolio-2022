ARCTURUS AUTO GROUP

ARCTURUS A.G. is an auto group named after the brightest star visible from Earth. The club welcomes enthusiasts from different car cultures. All members and their car builds are accepted as equally bright stars and respected without regard to their car or its value. The club also organizes car meets and track days to promote and support car culture and road safety.
