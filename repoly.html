RE:POLY SUSTAINABLE FABRIC BRANDING

More than 1 billion tons of food is wasted every year around the world. At the same time, polyester fabric releases microplastics as they are used and washed over time. RE:POLY is a textile company that offers a better alternative to help solve both problems. RE:POLY creates sustainable, biodegradable and carbon-neutral textiles from food waste for clothing brands around the world.
