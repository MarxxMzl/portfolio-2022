NORMA DESIGN CONFERENCE POSTER & COLLATERAL

A two-sided poster and stand-alone booklet for Norma, a fictional conference for designers. The brief design challenge in this student project was to correct typographic inconsistencies, and formatting errors, and to establish a strong sense of hierarchy that helps viewers easily navigate the event details.
